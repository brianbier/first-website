Who Am I?

I am a Full Stack developer and UX designer. I learned to program while I was attending the New York City Dev Bootcamp. I believe in simplicity, innovation, and creativity when designing and building web applications. When I am not cranking out elegant code or designing beautiful interfaces, you will find me riding my bike on the west side highway or drinking dark coffee at a local coffee shop.

What I am all about

New York is a beautiful place and especially during the winter months. Snowboarding is one of those sports I am not good at but I never give up. Occasionally you will find me at Thunder Ridge ski area as soon as the season begins. Although the majority of the year you will find me behind a computer screen, I do set aside time for family. Family is very important to me and I do believe everyone should take the time off in order to have a well-balanced life. Many people have played a key role in my career and I am very thankful. But I've also worked extremely hard to get to where I am today. Every challenge that I deal with on a day-to-day basis is always meaningful and rewarding. Bottom line: I love what I do and will continue doing what I love.
