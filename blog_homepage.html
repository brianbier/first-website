Enumerables, Enumerables, Enumerables, I have to say that I had a hard time saying this word and trying to understand what it means was just as difficult. The ruby documentation is great but when I read their definition of an Enumerable it made my head spin. Here is the ruby-doc definition “The enumeral mixin provides collection classes with several traversal and searching methods, and with the ability to sort. The class must provide a method each, which yields successive members of the collection. If Enumerable#max, #min, or #sort is used, the objects in the collection must also implement a meaningful <=> operator as these methods rely on an ordering between members of the collection.” Now if you understand what that means this blog is not for you.
